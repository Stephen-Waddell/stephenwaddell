"Stephen Waddell creates photographs that transcend the typical treatment of the medium. Though his scenes are often un-staged, captured spontaneously, and seem to present impromptu subject matter, Waddell’s framing, composition, colour, and lighting are far more akin to a meticulous historical paintings than off-the-cuff photographs. His discerning eye, precise captures, and references to our pictorial history are exactly what the artist is known for.""

-Stephen Waddell video interview by GalleriesWest
